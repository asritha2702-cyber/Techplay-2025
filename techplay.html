About TechPlay 2025

The Future of Sports Technology

TechPlay 2025 is the premier convergence of technology and athletics, bringing together visionary startups, tech leaders, and sports enthusiasts under one roof.

Experience the cutting edge of wearable technology, AI-powered training systems, and revolutionary fitness innovations that are reshaping how athletes train, compete, and recover.

Join us for three days of groundbreaking demonstrations, insightful keynotes, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities that will define the future of sports technology.
